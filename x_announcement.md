## 2. Educational post

Why does AI agent identity need Proof-of-Work?

An "agent identity" today is usually a wallet address anyone can create for free in seconds.

That means an attacker can mint 10,000 fake agents for ~$0 to manipulate reputation, airdrops, or agent-to-agent trust. Free identity = sybil-trivial.

PoW changes the economics. On HACD, every identity costs real mining + HAC-burning + a 50 HAC stack cost. Mass-fake-agent attacks stop being free.

That's the whole point: sybil resistance is an economic problem, and only PoW credibly solves it.

Bitcoin proved PoW for money. HACD brings PoW to assets.
